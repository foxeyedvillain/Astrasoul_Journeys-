Kedarnath, Uttarakhand

The Kedarnath Temple, located in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India, holds immense religious significance as one of the 12 Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva and a key site in the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage circuit.

Explore Kedarnath

Somnath,Gujarat

The Somnath Temple, located in Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Gujarat, India, is one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva and holds immense religious and historical significance. Its history spans thousands of years, with mentions in ancient scriptures such as the Rigveda, Shiva Purana, and Skanda Purana.

Explore Somnath

Haridwar is one of the oldest and most sacred cities in India, with a history deeply rooted in mythology, ancient civilizations, and successive empires.
